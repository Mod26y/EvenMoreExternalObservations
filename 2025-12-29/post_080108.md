Original Article: https://www.darkreading.com/application-security/dark-reading-opens-state-appsec-survey

1) What Happened: Dark Reading has initiated the State of Application Security Survey, inviting professionals to participate in gathering insights into the current trends, challenges, and strategies in application security. The survey aims to compile data from various industry experts to understand how organizations are addressing application security threats and vulnerabilities. This opens an opportunity for professionals to share their experiences and contribute to a comprehensive analysis regarding the state of application security.

2) Why It Matters: The results of the survey will provide critical insights into the evolving landscape of application security, highlighting prevalent vulnerabilities, common security practices, and emerging threats. Understanding these aspects is crucial for organizations to benchmark their security posture and identify areas for improvement. Participation in the survey can also foster a sense of community and collaboration among cybersecurity professionals, contributing to enhanced collective knowledge and sharing of best practices.

3) What Actions Should be Taken: Organizations should encourage their cybersecurity teams to participate in the survey to contribute invaluable insights from their experiences. Engaging in such surveys can help teams stay informed about industry trends and gain knowledge that could inform their security strategies. Additionally, once results are published, reviewing the findings can help organizations assess their application security protocols and make informed decisions on strengthening their defenses against evolving threats.
