Effective recovery plans ensure minimal service disruption and protect against financial losses and reputational damage. In healthcare, such outages can also threaten patient care and safety, emphasizing the need for robust cybersecurity measures.

3) Organizations should evaluate and enhance their disaster recovery strategies, ensuring they align with current threat landscapes and operational needs.
