1) OpenAI introduced ChatGPT Health, a dedicated platform for health-related conversations, which promises not to utilize private health information to train its AI models. ChatGPT Health aims to offer a secure space for health dialogues, prompting users during regular chats to switch to this space for health inquiries. Early access is being gradually provided to users through a waitlist. OpenAI emphasizes the importance of not considering ChatGPT's responses as medical advice, thereby acknowledging its role as a tool rather than a healthcare provider.
