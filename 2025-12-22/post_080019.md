Microsoft Teams experienced a service disruption starting at 14:30 PM ET, causing messaging delays and issues with other service functions across all regions, including the United States and Europe. Microsoft acknowledged the problem and reported observing recovery signs while continuing analysis to identify the cause and impacted scenarios. The service was reported fully recovered by 15:30 PM ET.

The incident is significant because it highlights the reliance on Microsoft Teams as a critical communication tool for organizations globally. Disruptions can impede operations, affect productivity, and hinder collaboration, especially for businesses and educational institutions that depend heavily on real-time messaging and conferencing tools.
