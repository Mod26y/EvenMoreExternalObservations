3) Organizations might consider diversifying their service providers to mitigate single points of failure. Regularly testing these plans ensures the ability to quickly switch to backups when necessary. This incident also encourages the assessment of current dependency on single providers and the evaluation of potential risks, reinforcing the importance of resilience planning in the digital economy.
