Original Article: https://www.bleepingcomputer.com/news/security/passwords-to-passkeys-staying-iso-27001-compliant-in-a-passwordless-era/

1) The article discusses the transition from password-based security systems to passkey technology, emphasizing its significance for organizations adhering to ISO/IEC 27001 compliance. Transitioning from traditional passwords to passkeys involves using cryptographic keys, biometrics, or possession-based factors to authenticate users. This shift is necessitated due to the vulnerabilities surrounding passwords, such as high rates of password reuse and compromised credentials leading to security breaches. It further outlines how organizations can align their passkey adoption with ISO/IEC 27001 controls and addresses challenges, benefits, and misconceptions related to this transition.

2) This transition matters because password-based authentication systems remain highly vulnerable, contributing to nearly half of all security incidents due to password compromises. The modern digital landscape demands more secure authentication methods, and passkeys offer a significant security upgrade by eliminating the cognitive burden of remembering passwords and mitigating risks like phishing and credential theft. Aligning passkey implementation with ISO/IEC 27001 compliance not only strengthens security posture but also ensures organizations meet leading security standards and frameworks, crucial for maintaining trust and compliance in today's interconnected world.

3) Organizations should take several actions based on this information. First, prioritize adopting passkeys for critical systems and privileged accounts, ensuring alignment with ISO/IEC 27001 requirements. Establish comprehensive policies for passkey management, including enrollment, recovery, and backup procedures, to mitigate risks like device loss. Educate users to recognize new threats like downgrade or phishing attacks. Implement robust monitoring and logging systems to maintain control and oversight during the transition. Finally, maintain thorough documentation throughout the process to support compliance audits and foster a culture of security awareness that evolves with technological advancements.
