Segmentation is a cybersecurity strategy that involves partitioning a network into isolated segments to contain threats, limiting the lateral movement of attackers.

**Why It Matters:**
The emphasis on operators' attention highlights that segmentation's effectiveness hinges on human factors as much as technology. Properly managed segmentation can thwart cyberattacks on critical infrastructure that OT systems often support. Inattention or misconfigurations can lead to vulnerabilities being exploited, causing disruptions. Given OT's integration into essential services like utilities, robust cybersecurity practices are crucial for operational integrity and public safety.

**Actions to Take:**
Organizations may benefit from increasing their personnel's awareness and training regarding OT network management. Regular audits and improvements of segmentation practices should be encouraged to identify potential issues. Enhanced monitoring tools can assist operators in keeping visibility over segmented networks. By prioritizing proactive and continuous education, companies can bolster defenses, binding technical measures with vigilant operational oversight.
