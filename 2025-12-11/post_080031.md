**1) What Happened:** Microsoft announced a forthcoming security feature for Microsoft Teams called the "External Domains Anomalies Report." This tool will analyze traffic patterns to identify suspicious interactions with external domains. By monitoring messaging trends, it will detect abnormal activities such as sudden spikes in communication, interactions with new domains, and unusual engagement patterns. This enhancement aims to safeguard organizations by providing administrators with insights into potential data-sharing risks, without hindering legitimate external communications. The feature is scheduled to roll out globally in February 2026 for standard multi-tenant environments on the web.
