The incident highlights vulnerabilities in critical infrastructure, emphasizing the need for robust cybersecurity measures.

3) Organizations using the CodeRED system should prioritize resetting and securing user credentials to prevent unauthorized access due to data exposure. Security personnel should review and enhance incident response procedures, emphasizing timely communication and restoration processes. Additionally, there should be an emphasis on cybersecurity training for users to understand the risks of password reuse. Affected agencies might also consider raising awareness among residents about potential phishing risks associated with the data breach. Ongoing monitoring of the situation is vital, along with collaboration with cybersecurity professionals to mitigate potential threats.
