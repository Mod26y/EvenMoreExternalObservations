1) What happened: Full Sail University is launching a Cyber Defense Range on its campus, an initiative powered by IBM, AWS, and Cloud Range. This facility will provide students with hands-on experience in simulating real-world cyber-defense operations. It incorporates state-of-the-art technologies to offer robust training environments where individuals can practice defending against cybersecurity threats. The partnership signifies a collaborative effort in bridging the skills gap in cybersecurity by providing practical learning opportunities in a controlled environment.

2) Why it matters: Cybersecurity is a critical concern globally, and there is a growing demand for skilled professionals in this field. By establishing a cyber defense range, Full Sail University addresses this need by offering students tangible skills and experience that are highly valued in the industry. This facility also demonstrates the importance of collaboration between academia and industry leaders like IBM and AWS, ensuring educational programs align with current technological advancements and industry expectations in cybersecurity.

3) What actions should be taken: Other educational institutions might consider developing similar facilities or partnerships to enhance cybersecurity training programs. County IT departments could leverage such partnerships to offer continuous education and training for their staff. Establishing local collaboration with universities could serve as a pipeline for talent, ensuring a steady influx of well-trained cybersecurity professionals ready to tackle emerging threats. Additionally, organizations should explore integrating similar hands-on training experiences to enhance their teams' readiness and responsiveness to cyber incidents.
