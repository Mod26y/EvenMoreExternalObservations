APTs are sophisticated; their focus on critical infrastructure highlights vulnerabilities within essential systems. Identifying and understanding these threats is crucial for developing defense mechanisms and enhancing cybersecurity protocols to protect sensitive information and maintain the integrity of vital services that society relies upon.
