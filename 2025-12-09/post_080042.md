**2) Why it matters?**

The incident underscores the complex threats posed by advanced hacking tools which, despite being intended for security research and educational purposes, can be misused for malicious activities. This case highlights vulnerabilities in national defense systems and the importance of vigilance against cyber threats. The widespread availability and relatively low cost of these devices increase the risk of cyber attacks on critical infrastructure. Such incidents could inspire stricter regulation of hacking tools and necessitate international cooperation to manage transnational cyber threats, thus emphasizing the need for robust cybersecurity measures within national borders.

**3) What actions should be taken as a result of this information?**

In response to this event, authorities may consider enhancing border security protocols to better identify and intercept suspicious individuals or equipment. Increased collaboration between international cybersecurity agencies could improve intelligence sharing and collective defensive strategies. Additionally, raising public awareness about the potential criminal use of legal hacking tools may deter misuse and promote vigilant reporting of suspicious activities by the public.
