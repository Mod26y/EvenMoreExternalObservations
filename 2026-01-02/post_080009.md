Original Article: https://www.bleepingcomputer.com/news/artificial-intelligence/openai-is-offering-20-chatgpt-plus-for-free-to-some-users/

1) What happened: OpenAI is providing a one-month free ChatGPT Plus subscription to current subscribers who attempt to cancel their $20/month subscription. This promotional offer is designed to retain current users. The offer is gradually being rolled out in multiple regions, and once the free period ends, normal subscription billing resumes unless canceled. Users have to check their subscription under the "Manage" button in the payment section to understand their plan status after the promotional period ends.

2) Why it matters: From a cybersecurity perspective, this development highlights the necessity for organizations to evaluate new software and service terms, especially regarding payments and subscriptions. It draws attention to the importance of managing digital service subscriptions securely to avoid unexpected charges or services. Severely impacting users' readiness to engage with AI tools, promotions like these indicate strategic efforts to maintain user bases amid growing competition and alert organizations to potential changes in service agreements or user data handling.

3) What actions should be taken: Organizations should ensure that employees are aware of the implications of accepting promotional offers, especially regarding software used in professional settings. It is crucial to review user agreements, understand the billing cycle, and ensure automatic billing adjustments are accurately reflected in financial records. Additionally, companies might consider establishing guidelines for the secure management of digital service subscriptions to mitigate risks associated with unauthorized payments or breaches stemming from less scrutinized service use.
