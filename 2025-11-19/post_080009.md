The recent update in Thunderbird version 145 includes native support for Microsoft Exchange accounts via the Exchange Web Services (EWS) protocol. This integration allows Thunderbird users within Exchange-hosted environments, such as Microsoft 365, to access email services without relying on additional third-party add-ons. The update simplifies processes like message synchronization and folder management, providing a smoother transition for users migrating from Outlook to Thunderbird.

This development emphasizes enhanced interoperability in email clients, broadening user choices beyond traditional Outlook options. As many organizations use Microsoft Exchange, Thunderbird's native support offers an alternative that could lead to more flexible, cost-effective IT strategies while expanding options for handling email services securely and efficiently.
