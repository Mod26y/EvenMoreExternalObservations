This fusion of cyber and physical criminal activities underscores the increasing complexity of threats faced by supply chains. Moreover, it highlights the need for integrated cybersecurity measures within industries traditionally focused on physical security, as vulnerabilities in digital infrastructure can be exploited to compromise physical assets.

3) What actions should be taken:
Organizations involved in logistics and supply chain management could benefit from employing a holistic security approach, integrating both cyber and physical security measures.
