This alliance focuses on integrating cybersecurity within day-to-day operations to maintain operational excellence. The collaboration ensures that security initiatives are aligned with business objectives, potentially reducing the risk of disruptions caused by cyber threats and enhancing the organization's resilience against attacks.

2) This partnership matters because it highlights the importance of a cohesive approach to cybersecurity, where operational leaders and security experts work together to preemptively address vulnerabilities.
