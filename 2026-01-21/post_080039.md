Original Article: https://www.bleepingcomputer.com/news/artificial-intelligence/openai-rolls-out-age-prediction-model-on-chatgpt-to-detect-your-age/

1) What happened: OpenAI has introduced an age prediction model for ChatGPT to determine users' ages and apply safety restrictions, specifically targeting underage users. This model assesses users' interactions and usage times to categorize them as adults or teenagers, potentially limiting access to specific content deemed inappropriate for minors.

2) Why it matters: The introduction of this feature is significant as it reflects growing concerns and responsibilities surrounding the exposure of inappropriate or harmful content to minors. The model aims to protect younger audiences from adult content, thereby aligning with ethical standards and legal compliance regarding online safety for underage users.

3) What actions should be taken: Organizations using ChatGPT should assess how this change might affect their users and ensure that they have clear communication regarding the new age detection process. They could provide guidance on age verification methods while ensuring compliance with privacy policies. Additionally, stakeholders might want to monitor developments from OpenAI pertaining to the AI's accuracy to address false identifications promptly.
