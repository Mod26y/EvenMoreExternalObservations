**1) What happened:**  
Google's new large language model, Gemini 3, has been spotted on AI Studio, suggesting its imminent release. AI Studio serves as a platform for developers, researchers, and students to build applications using the Gemini models. Currently, the Gemini 2.5 Pro is in use, but Gemini 3 is expected to offer enhancements such as better control over parameters like temperature, which influences reasoning capabilities. Indications of Gemini 3 have also appeared on Vertex AI, a cloud platform by Google, pointing to a significant upcoming release.
