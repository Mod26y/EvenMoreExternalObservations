NAKIVO has released version 11.1 of its Backup & Replication software, which includes several new features and enhancements. These updates focus on improving disaster recovery capabilities, with notable features like automated real-time replication, expanded language support, and granular backups for physical machines. The software now supports Proxmox VE with VM replication and instant recovery, enhancing business continuity without costly infrastructure changes. Additionally, a new MSP Direct Connect feature facilitates secure communications without client-side port openings, marking a step towards streamlined management of hybrid infrastructures.

This release is significant as it enhances the resilience and efficiency of IT operations across diverse environments, crucial for maintaining continuity and minimizing data loss and downtime. The real-time replication and instant recovery features are particularly important for businesses requiring uninterrupted services. Furthermore, the secure and streamlined connection for MSPs underscores evolving needs for simplified and secure client-management interactions, pertinent as businesses increasingly embrace hybrid infrastructures and remote solutions.

Organizations using NAKIVO solutions can evaluate the potential benefits of these new features for their infrastructure, especially focusing on enhanced replication and recovery options to bolster their disaster recovery strategies. It would be beneficial to assess the compatibility of NAKIVO v11.1 with existing systems, focusing on transitioning to more secure and efficient MSP management if applicable. Moreover, leveraging the free trial can offer insights into how the upgrades fit organizational operations, allowing for informed decisions regarding further adoption or procedural adjustments.
